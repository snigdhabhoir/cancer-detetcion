Understanding Breast Cancer Types

Benign Tumor

A benign tumor is non-cancerous and doesn't spread. However, monitoring is essential as it can still affect breast health.

Malignant Tumor

A malignant tumor is cancerous and can invade nearby tissues. Early detection is crucial for treatment and prognosis.
